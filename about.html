Our Story

Founded in 2025, Cloud Cakes began with a simple dream: to create desserts that taste as heavenly as they look. Our journey started in a small kitchen with big aspirations, and today, we're proud to be Aurangabad's premier destination for custom cakes and sweet celebrations.

Every cake we create is a masterpiece of flavor, artistry, and love. We believe in using only the finest ingredients, combining traditional baking methods with innovative designs to create cakes that are truly unforgettable.
